   - should <-> ought to
   - will/would <-> be going to
   - can/could <-> be able to
   - may/might <-> be likely to

* 不熟的地方
   - 国三的 介词
   - as ... as 惯用表现
     1. as busy as a bee 非常忙碌
     2. as cool as a cucumber 极端冷静
     3. as cunning as a fox 十分狡猾
     4. as dead as a doornail 了无生息
     5. as flat as a pancake 非常平坦
     6. as sweet as honey 非常甜美
     7. as like as two peas 非常相似
   - such 和 so 的用法
   - as...as ever 依旧
     - She looks as happy as ever. 她看起来依旧很快乐
   - not so much as
      - 引用教程地址 https://kiki888.pixnet.net/blog/post/33271782-not-so-much...as...---%E8%88%87%E5%85%B6%E8%AA%AA%E6%98%AF%E2%80%A6%E2%80%A6%E5%80%92%E4%B8%8D%E5%A6%82%E8%AA%AA%E6%98%AF%E2%80%A6
      - 与其说是...不如说是...
         - 语法
            - not so adj/adv. as adj./adv.
            - not so much n. as n.
            - not so much 介词短语/不定式短语 as 介词短语/不定式短语
            - not so much v. as v.
         - 例句
            - It was not so much a crime as a mistake. 与其说是犯罪，不如说是过失
               - 这里的 so much .. as 不能和 as..as 的方式进行理解
               - It was rather a mistake than a crime.
               - It was a mistake rather than a crime.
            - I don't feel angry so much as sad. # 与其说我愤怒，不如说我悲伤
      - not so much as...  /  without so much as... （就连...也没有 或 就连...也不）
         - 一些语法
            - cannot so much as 原型动词 # 甚至...都不能
            - do not so much as 原型动词 # 甚至...都不
            - have not so much as p.p.   # 甚至...都没有
         - 举例
            - The man didn't so much as apologize to me. 这个男人根本没有根我道歉
               - The man didn't
               - so much as 修饰 apologize
               - apologize # v. 道歉
               - to me 修饰 apologize
            - He left me without so much as saying thanks. 他道谢都没有就离开了
   - as~as any + 单数名词（像任何人一样...）
     - He is as hardworking as any (person) in the village. 他像村子里任何人一样努力工作
       - 因为 as ... as 中间必须是 形容词或副词，所以不能把 work hard 带进行
   - as many/much as + 数词（多达...）
     - As many as fifty thousand birds spend the winter here. 有多达5万只鸟在此过冬
     - I paid as much as two hundred yuan for the sweater. 这件毛衣我付了多达200元
     - Radio broadcasting started as early as 1920. 广播早在1920年就开始了
     - He goes to movies as often as three times a mounth. 他一个月看电影次数高达三次
   - as many/much ... （同数/量的）（一样的多）
     1. I found six mistakes in as many lines. 我在六行里找到了六个错误
        - 因为两处用到了 six，本着减少重复的特点而使用此语法
     2. The dour group visited five cities (as many days)|(in five days). 旅行团在五天之内造访了五个城市
   - as good as...（几乎等于）（几乎根..一样）
     1. This house is a great buy.It's as good as new. 这个房子是一笔好买卖，几乎等于是新的（其实不是新的）
     2. His car is as good as a piece of junk. 这辆车几乎是片垃圾
   - What if it rains? 万一下雨怎么办
     - What if # 万一
   - 连词
      - 连词可以连接，名词，片语，子句（比如 not only...but also... | but | and | or | but 等）
      - 间接问句
         - I have no idea who that tall girl is. 我对于那个女孩是谁一无所知
           - who... 作 idea 的同位语
      - 名词片语 可以作 主词，受词，补语
         - Who to bell the cat is a big problem. # 谁担当这危险的任务是个大问题
           - 名词片语作主语
         - The problem is who to bell the cat. # 这个问题是谁担当这危险的任务
           - 名词片语作补语
         - We don't know who to bell the cat is a big problem. # 我不知道谁担当这个危险的任务
           - 名词片语作受词
      - as 有的很多意思：像，作为，当，因为，虽然
      - whether 和 if 表示“是否”时，如果这个“是否”在 句首 或 be动词后充当补语，则只能用 whether
      - 一些词组
         - be apt to（易于...的），这是一种比较倾于某种情况的形式
            - We are apt to make mistakes.（我们容易犯错）（虽然easy也是“容易”的意思，但是easy是对于事务的，但是这里的容易是一种倾向性的）
         - be ready to v.（愿意~，易于~）
            - I am ready to help you.（我愿意帮助你）
               - ready 是形容词
            - He is too ready suspect anything.（我太怀疑你）
         - be willing to（乐意~）（这里的 willing 是形容词）
            - I am willing to play games with you.（我乐意和你玩游戏）
         - be eager to（渴望）或 hunger for + n
            - He is eager to see you.（他想见你）
         - be likely to（可能）
            - It is likely to rain soon.（快要下雨了）
         - be anxious to（急着想要）
            - He is anxious to go home.（他急着回家）
         - enough to v（够~可以~）
            - 等价于 so~that + S + (can) ~（如此...以至于...）

* 极其容易弄混的情况
   1. only if => 只要...（形成条件）
      - only if you pass the examination and become a college student,will I accept you as a friend. 只要你通过考试并成为一个大学生，我才会接受你作朋友
   2. if only + 假设法过去式/假设法过去完成式 => 比 wish 更强烈的希望
      - If only I had money,I could buy some new clothes. 要是我有钱，我就可以买新衣服了
      - If only my tooth would stop hurting. 但愿我的牙齿不疼了
   3. have only to v. 只要做...就行了
      - You have only to follow his counsel. 你只要遵守他的建议就行了
      - have only to v. 和 only if 的区别：前者是一种强调，后者是一种假设条件
   4. as if / as though 宛如，好像
      - He talks as if he knew everything. 他说得好像他知道一切一样
   5. by 介词的多种意义
      - 在...时间之后
      - 被...
      - 在...旁边
      - He is taller than I by three inches. 他比我高三寸（表示程度，单位，比例）

* 动词
   - 授予动词
      - 授予动词 + 直接受词：He give me a book.
      - 授予动词 + 介词 + 间接动词 + 直接受词 He give a book to me.

   - 连缀动词
      - 其后可以根形容词，作用与 beV 的一般作用有类似功能
      - feel, taste, keep, taste 等
      - The man looked angry. 这个男人看起来很生气
      - Come home before it gets dark. 天黑前回家

   - 感观动词（有原型不定式的情况）
      - 眼，耳，口，手，鼻
      - S + V + O + v./ving/p.p.
      - I saw him mow/mowing the lawn. 我看到他在除草
         - 如果是 mow 这种原型只是一般的表述，已经经过了这个事情
         - 如果是 mowing 这种分词表明是动作的伴随

   - 使吏动词（有原型不定式的情况）
      - get + O + to v./p.p.
      - let + O + v./be p.p.
      - make/have + O + v./p.p.
      - 比较特殊的：help + O + (to) v.

   - 情绪动词
      - 句法
         - S（事物） + 情绪V + O 人
         - S（事物） + beV + 情绪Ving + to + O（人）
         - S（人）+ beV + 情绪p.p. + 介词 + O（事物）
      - 举例
         - The computer games interests young people.
         - The computer games is interesting to young people.
         - Young people are interested in the computer game.
      - 情绪动词
         - interested in 对...有兴趣
         - surprised at 对...感到惊讶
         - satisfied with 对...感到满意
         - impressed with/by 对...有印象
         - pleased with 欣喜于...
         - excited about 激动于...
         - worried about 对...感到担忧


- 句型

   - S + V + C(=>分词）
      - 可做为 go,come,keep,lie,sit,stand,walk 等动词之后的主词补语，用来表示伴随着的动作
         - We stood looking at the monkeys. 我们站着看猴子
            - 不能是 look standing，因为主要动词是 stand，look 只是伴随的情况
      - 可放在 appear,seem,look,become,feel,get,sit,lie,remain 等字的后面用来表示动作所产生的结果（起连缀动词的作用）
         - The house appeared(=> seemed/looked) deserted. 那栋房子看起来像是荒废了似的

   - S + V + O + C(=>分词)
      - 可做为感官动词及 keep,leave,set,start,catch,find 等动词之后的受词补语
         - He left the engine be running. 他让引擎运转着（指状态，分词有形容词的概念）
         - He let her daughter watch TV. 他让他女儿看电视（指动作，使役动词）
         - We heard the rain beating against the window. 我听到雨水拍打着窗户
         - We heard the rain beat against the window. 我听到了雨水拍打着窗户


* 副词
   - 情状副词
      - 情状副词位置不同其意义也不一样，情状副词是描述情况与状态的
      - be + 情况副词 + Ving
      - He was anxiously waiting for her arrival. 他很焦急地等待着她到来

   - 程度副词 => so（如此地）, too（太）, very（非常）, quite（相当）, enough（足够）... 等
      - 注意 very 修饰 形容词的原级   形容词的最高级 现在分词 （不能修饰一般动词）
      - 注意 much 修饰 形容词的比较级 形容词的最高级 过去分词 一般动词
      - 其它例外情况举例
         - very different
         - much the same
      - He is much the best boy of all.
      - He is the very best boy of all.

* 关系代名词
   1. 必须用 that 的情况
      - 定义
         1. 先行词有序数
         2. 先行词有最高级
         3. 先行词充当人和物的共同
         4. 先行词前有 which, who 作疑问词时，避免引起混乱
         5. 先行词有 all,no,every,any,~thing,~one,~body 的情况
         6. 先行词前有 the only, the same, the very 时
         7. There/Here be... 时，关系代名词可省略（先行词 前/后 面有 There/here be），一般是作为从句的受词才可省略

   2. 特殊情况
      1. He looked for a car. The engine of the car was in good condition
          - 等价：He looked for a car the engine of which was in good condition.
             - 这里的 which 实际上是关系代名词，并不是所有的关系代名词都是挨着先行词的
          - 等价：He looked for a car of which the engine was in good condition.
             - 实际上是：He looked for a car which the engine was in good condition of.（of 后面是关系代名词 which）
      2. I don't like the way that he talks.
         - I don't like the way that he talks.
         - I don't like that he talks.
         - I don't like he talks.
         - 注意：I don't like his talking.（这种形式有什么区别）
      3. There/Here be... 时，关系代名词可省略（先行词 前/后 面有 There/here be），一般是作为从句的受词才可省略
         - There is someone (who) wants to see you. 有人想要见你
         - This is the only bow tie (that) there is in the store. 这里是这家店里唯一的一个蝴蝶结

   3. 特殊情况2
      - 关系代名词中：what = (all that)/(the thing(s) that)/(anything that)
         - What I want is of peace of mind. 我所要的是心灵的平静
      - what is called = what we(=>you, they) call 所谓的...
         - He is what is called working dictionary. 他是所谓的活字典
      - A is to ~ what B is to ~（A之对~的关系正如B之对于~的关系）
         - Air is to us what water is to fish. 空气之于人犹如水之如鱼
      - What A is/was/used to be（今日的A / 昔日的A / 昔日的A）
         - She is not what she was ten years ago. 她不再是十年前的她
      - what is 比较级 更加...的是；而且...
         - He is a bright what boy,(and) what is more,he is kindhearted. 他是个阳光的男孩，而且，他很热心
         - They lost their way,what was worse,night was coming on. 他迷路了，更糟糕的，夜晚将至
      - what with ~ and (what with) ~ 一则因~；一则因~
         - What with overwork and (what with) poor meals,she fell ill. 一则因过量工作，一则因应为营养不良，她生病了

   4. 特殊情况3
      1. 关系副词一般有：where,when,why,how（通常 how 通常没有明确的先行词）
      2. 注意关系副词的先行词通常是可以省略的
         - This is (the day) when the garbage truck come. 这是垃圾车来的日子
      3. that 有时可做为关系副词代替 where,when,why 等，通常代替 when 的情形较多，此时 that 可省略（这种情况相当于是作了 how 的作用，因为 how 通常没有先行词）
         - 我几乎不记得跟她头一次网页的日子
            - Little do I remember the day I first met her.
            - Little do I remember (that) I first met her.

   5. 复合关系词
      1. 关系代名词 + ever 任何...
         - 词例
            - whoever = anyone who; whomever = anyone who(m)
            - whichever = any one that; whatever = anything that
            - whenever = at any time when
            - wherever = at any place where
         - 例子
            - He tried to help any one that was in need of help. 他设法帮助需要帮助的任何的人
               - He tried to help whoever was in need of help.
            - Help yourself to any one that you want. 自行取用你想要的东西
               - Help yourself to whichever you want.
            - I'll treat today.You can order anything that you like. 今天我请客，你可以点任何你喜欢的菜
               - I'll treat today.You can order whatever you like.

      2. 复合关系词可以表示让步=> 无论...
         - 词例
            - whoever = no matter who
            - whichever = no matter which
            - whatever = no matter what
            - whenever = no matter when
            - wherever = no matter where
            - however = no matter how
         - 例子
            - Whoever is calling,I don't want to answer the phone. 无论是谁打电话来，我都不想接
            - Whatever happens,I always love you. 无论发生什么，我将永远爱你
            - I'll be thinking at you wherever I am. 无论我在什么地方，我都会想起你

   6. 关系形容词（连词 + 形容词）
         - I gave the child all the little money that I had then. 我给了那个小孩我那里所有的零钱 
            - I gave the child all the little money.（分句1）
            - I had all the little money then.（分句2）
            - all the little money 用 that 代替
            - I gave the child all the little money that I had then.
               - all the 是修饰 little money 的形容词
               - all the 和 that 结合得到 what（由 关系代名词和先行词的结合 可知）
               - 等价：I gave the child what little money I had then.
         - For ten days,in/during which time he kept indoors,nobody visited him.
            - 有十天之久，在这段期间他一直待在家，没人来走访
            - which 是 time 的形容词，还是连接词
         - We may miss the train,in which case we'll be late for the appointment.
            - 我们可能错过火车，如此一来我们的约会就会迟到


* 虚拟语气

   2. if only + S + 假设法过去式/假设法过去完成式（但愿；希望）

         - She was kind enough to offer me her seat. 她好心让位给我（这里的 enough 是副词）

         - so 副词
      - I am not such a fool as to trust him. 我没有愚蠢到去相信他
         - such 形容词

   14. 不定式的特殊情况
      1. 情况1（只能用 to + v. 的不定式）
         - 词型
            1. 表希望，期待：like, want, wish, expect 等
            2. 表许可：allow, permit
            3. 表思考，认为：think, believe, consider, find, recognize 等
            4. 表依赖，建议，要求，命令：
               - tell(告诉), advise(建议), order(命令), ask(要求), request(要求), require(要求), force(强迫，迫使), persuade(说服), get(叫), cause(使), teach(教), beg(企求，请求) 等
         - 例子
            - I want you to tell the truth. 我想你说实话
            - Please allow me to introduce my wife,Rosaline. 请允许我介绍我的妻子 Rosaline
            - We believe him to be honest 我认为他是诚实的
            - The doctor advised him to stop smoking. 医生建议过他戒烟
      2. 情况2（动词用原型，或分词的情况）
         - 感官动词（感观动词也可以是 ing 形式，表示动作进行）
            - 词型：眼，耳，鼻，舌，皮肤
               - see 一般的看 不能用于 进行式（瞬时动词）
               - watch 比较长时间地看（连续动词）
               - look at 比较仔细地看（连续动词）
               - notice 注意看
               - observe 观察地看
               - hear 一般地听，下一秒可能就没有听了，不能用于 进行式
               - listen to 仔细地听
         - 举例
            - I felt my heart beat violently. 我感觉我的心脏跳得很厉害
      3. 使役动词之后也是根原型：make, have, let, bid, have
         - 使役动词后的动词用 原型
         - 注意：get sb. to + v. 不定式
         - The mom made the boy turn off TV. 妈妈让那个孩子关掉电视
         - I got him to repair the radio. 我叫他修理这个收音机
      4. S + v. + it + ... + to v. 不定式（这里的 it 是假受词）
         - find, think, make, consider
         - She found that it is impossible to understand him. 他发现理解它是不可能的
            - She found it impossible to understand him.
      5. seem（似乎）, appear（似乎）, happen（碰巧）, prove（结果是，证明是） 等 + to v.
         - S + seem[appear] + adj./to v. => It seems(appears) that + S + v.
         - I happened to meet one of my friends = It happened that I meeted one of my friends.
      6. 独立不定词
         - to speak strictly 严格来说
         - to be frank(with you) 坦白说
         - to begin with 一开始
         - to tell the truth 老实说
         - to be honest 诚实说
         - to make matters worse 更糟的是
         - to be sure 的确
         - not to say + adj. 即使不能说是~
      7. 不定词的惯用表现
         - had better v. 最好...
         - would rather v. than v. 宁愿~而不愿~
         - do nothing but v. 什么都不做，只知道做...
         - choose/want + nothing but to v. 除了...其他都不想
         - expect/desire nothing but to v. 除了...其他都不期待
         - cannot but v. = cannot help Ving = cannot help but v. 忍不住...
         - have only to v. 只要...就行了
         - used to v. 过去习惯...，往常经常做...
         - be used to Ving. 现在习惯...
         - be used to v. 被用于做...
      8. 动名词
         - enjoy Ving. 喜欢.../享受...
         - give up Ving. 放弃...
      9. hardly, scarcely, rarely, seldom
         - hardly 几乎不
         - scarcely 几乎不
         - rarely 不多，稀少
         - seldom 不常，不多
      10. 一些不需要 be 的被动的动名词（因为是表现事务的本身的性质）
         - This watch needs repairing 这块表需要维修
         - The child wants scolding. 这个小孩需要被骂
         - He deserves helping. 他值得被帮助

   15. 特殊的虚拟语气
      1. wish + S + 假设法过去式/假设法过去完成式
         1. I wish I knew Jane's tell phone number. 我希望我知道 Jane 的电话号码（事实是我不知道）
         2. I wish I hadn't hought such an expensive dress then. 我但愿那时候我没有买那么贵的洋装（事实是我已经买了）
      2. It's time/about time/almost time/hight time + (that) + S + 假设法过去式
         - It's time 是时候...了（更注重事情）
         - It's about time 是...的时候了（更注重时间）
         - It's time that the children gone to bed. 该是小孩睡觉的时候了
            - 等于：It's time for the children to go to bed.
         - It's about time you apologized to her for what you did. 该是你为你做的事情向她道歉的时候了
            - 等于：It's about time for you to apologize to her for what you did.
      3. If only + S + 假设法过去式/假设法过去完成式
         - If only 和 I wish 表面意思差不多，都有”但愿，希望“的意思，但是 if only 的意愿要更强烈一些
         - If only my tooth would stop hurting! 但愿我的牙齿不疼了！
         - If only I had taken her advice. 但愿那时我有接受她的忠告
      4. as if 或 as though + S + 假设法过去式/假设法过去完成式
         1. He talks as if he knew everything. 他说得好像他知道一切的样子
         2. She looks as if she had looked a ghost. 她看起来好像她那时见到了鬼一样
      5. If it were not for ~ 若没有...的话 => 假设法过去式 / If it had not been for ~ 若没有...的话 => 假设法过去完成式
         - If it were not for music,he couldn't live a moment. 如果没有音乐，他一刻也活不下去
         - If it had not been for the traffic jam,we would have been in time for the game. 要是要时没有塞车，我们就能及时赶上比赛了
         
   16. 否定相关
      1. quite（相当地）, altogether（完全地）, completely（完全地）, entirely（完全地）, exactly（的确地） 等和 not 连用时 => 部分否定
      2. both 和 all 存在的否定是部分否定（both 是数量为二，all 是数量在二以上）
      3. neither 表示两者都不，none 表示全都不（一般用在数量为三或以上的情况）
      3. any longer 和 any more
         - no longer和 not any longer 都可以翻译为 “不再” 的意思
         - any longer 和 any more 的区别
            - 两者都可以表示 “不再继续”
            - not ... any more 是强调数量和程度的不再增加，表示再也不重复做某件事
            - not ... any longer 是强调动作或时间不再延续，表示再也不再继续做某件事
      4. 看起来是否定的肯定
         - You cannot/never be too careful when you drive at night. 当你在晚上开车时要小心
            - cannot 是否定，too 是“太”的极限，看起来是整体是否定的，但是整体是肯定的
         - You are not too old to learn. 你不能太老而不学习（活到老学到老）
      5. 不用否定词的否定
         - Who knows what will happen tomorrow? 谁知道明天会发生什么呢？（即没有人知道）
         - This book was too boring for me to read through. 这本书对我来说太无聊而不能读完
         - He would be the last person to tell a lie. 他绝不会说谎（他会是说谎的最后一个人）（从意思的理解他是绝不会说谎的人）
         - The story he told me was anything but boring. 他告诉我的是有趣的事情（他告诉我的是除了无聊的任何事情）（不无聊则是有趣）
         - Five hundred dollars for a pair of shoes is far from cheap! 五百美元的一驭鞋很贵（五百美元的一双鞋离偏宜很远）
         - This district is free from air pollution. 这个区域很干净（这个区域免于空气污染）
         - The view from the windows was beautiful beyond description. 从这个窗户看出去的景色美到难以形容（从这个窗户看出去的景色是在能够描述之外的美丽）
         - This book is quite above me. 这本书很难（这本书相当在我之上）
         - He is above cheating on a test. 他考试不作弊（他考试在作弊之上）
         - The alarm clock failed to go off. 闹钟不会响（这个闹钟响失败了）
         - Heavy fog kept us from driving fast. 浓雾使得我们免于开快车（浓雾使得我们远离开快车）
         - You had better fefrain from alcohol. 你最好避免喝酒
         - I know better than to do such a thing. 我不会笨到去做那样的事情（比起做那样的事情我更清楚）
         - We tried to open the door,but in vain. 我试图开门，却无果（vain 是“枉然”的意思）
       6. rob + 人 of 物品（抢了某人的物品）；steal + 物 from 人（人的物品被偷了 或 偷了某人的物品）
       7. dream,sleep,live,die,fight,smile 等 都是既可以作动词又可以作名词的词 => 同族受词


   17. 比较
      1. 原级的比较
         1. as 原级（adj. 或 adv.） as 与...一样...
            - He is as great as his elder brother. 他和他哥哥一样伟大
               - 这里的 great 是形容词
            - He is as great a person as his elder brother. 他是和他哥哥一样伟大的人
               - 两个 as 之间必须是 adj. 或 adv. ，但是这里的有 great，但也形容词了名词，所以成立
         2. not as(=>so) 原级 as ~ 不像...一样
            - He does not work as/so hard as his elder brother (does). 他不像他哥一样努力工作
         3. as many/much + n. + as 像一样多的
            - she has as many dresses as her elder sister (does). 她有和她姐姐一样多的洋装
         4. as many/much as + 数词 多达...
            - As many as fifty thousand birds spend the winter here. 有多达5万只鸟在此过冬
            - I paid as much as two hundred yuan for the sweater. 这件毛衣我付了多达 200 元
         5. as many/much ... （同 数/量 的）（一样的多）
            - I found six mistakes in as many lines. 我在六行里找到了六个错误（as many 指的就是前面的 six）
            - The dour group visited five cities as many days. 旅行团在五天之内行文了五个城市
         6. ~ times as ... as ... 多...倍
            - The bridge is half times as long as this one. 那卒桥长度是这座桥的一半
         7. as ... as possible (one can) 像...一样可能地
            - Call the police as soon as possible. 尽快报警
         8. not so much ~ as ~ 与其说是...不如说是...
            - It was not so much a crime as a mistake. 与其说是犯罪，不如说是过失
         9. not so much as  / without so much as 就连...也没有 或 就连...也不
            - The man didn't so much as apologize to me. 这个男人根本没有根我道歉
            - He left me without so much as saying thanks. 他道谢都没有就离开了
         10. as ... as ever 依旧
            - He looks as happy as ever. 她看起来依旧很快乐
         11. as ... as any + 单数名词 像任何xx一样...
            - He is as hardworking as any person in the village. 他像村子里的人一样努力工作
         12. as good as 几乎等于...；几乎根...一样
            - His car is as good as a piece of junk. 这辆车几乎是片垃圾
            - This house is a great buy.It's as good as new. 这个房子是一笔好买卖，几乎等于是新的


      2. 比较级的比较
         1. more ... than ... 与其...不如...
            - She was more sad than angry. 她与其说是生气不如说是难过
         2. 比较级 and 比较级
            - It's getting darker and darker. 天色变得越来越黑了
            - Entrance exams are becoming more and more difficult. 大学联 考越来越难了
         3. the 比较级 ~ the 比较级 ~ 越...就越...
            - The older we grow,the weaker our memory becomes. 年级越大，我们掋 记忆力就越差
            - The longer I waited the less patient I became. 我等得越久，我就越没有耐心
         4. (all) the 比较级 for 名词 / because ~ 因为 ... 因此便（更）...
            - We respect him (all) the more for his honesty. 因为诚实我们更尊敬他
               - We respect him (all) the more because he's honest. 因为诚实我们更尊敬他
         5. much less ~ 更不用说...了
            - I don't like mathematics,much less physics. 我不喜欢数学，更不用说物理了
            - The old man can't even walk,much less run. 这个老人甚至不能工作，不不用说跑了
         6. more or less 多多少少；sooner or later 迟早
         7. know better than ~ 不会傻到...
            - I know better than to go sailing without a life jacket. 我不会傻到航行不穿救生衣
         8. no more than 等表现不（2560 ～ 2570 line）


      3. 最高级的比较
         1. 最高级 in ~；最高级 of ~；最高级 + that + S + V
            - 注意在指定一定范围的时候要加 the
            - February is the shortest of all the months. 二月是所有月份中最短的
            - He drives the most carefully of us all. 他是我们全部人中开车最小心的
            - This is the finest view that I've ever seen. 这个是我曾经看过的景色中最好的
         2. 强调最高级 => very,much,by far,(far and away) 离得远
            - She is much the best singer in the country. 她是这个国家最出类拔萃的歌手
            - She is the very best singer in the country. 她是这个国家最出类拔萃的歌手
            - 注意 用 very 和 much 时 the 的位置
         3. even + 最高级 甚至连...
            - Even the richest man in the world can't buy her heart. 甚至全世界最富有的人都不能买到他的心
            - Even the most talented student in ourclass wasn't able to answer it. 甚至我们班最有才能的人也不能回答它
         4. at (the) most/best 顶多
            - It will take at (the) most/best three hours to reach our destination. 顶多花三个小时到达目的地
         5. at (the) least 最少；at (the) worst 最差
            - It will take at (the) least three hours to reach our destination. 最少花三个小时到达目的地
         6. make the most of  / make the best of 尽全力
            - make the most of （是在可能或确定有那个能力的情况下的尽全力，比较有利的情况下）
            - make the best of （尽其所能，比较没有利或不确定是否有利的情况）
            - I hope you will make the most of your abilities. 我希望你会充分利用你的能力
            - He made the best of his small income. 他充分利用了他最微薄的收入

      - 易混乱的词
         - 词
            - 生产，忍受 bear-bore-borne
            - 出生 bear-bore-born
         - 造句
            - My mother has borne four children. 我母亲生了四个小孩
            - He was born on january first(st). 他出生于一月一日
      - be marryied 结婚
         - marry 的原意是“嫁，娶”，用 married 表现结婚的状态
         - They have been married for seven years. 他们结婚七年了
      - be injured/wounded/be hurt 受伤
         - 词
            - be hurt 一般用于心灵或精神上的伤害
            - be wounded 一般用于刀枪物理的伤害
            - be injured 没有区分什么伤，可以通用
         - 举例
            - She was badly/slightly injured in the accident. 他因为事故受伤严重/轻微
            - He was wounded in the left leg. 他伤了左胳膊
            - Aren't you hurt? 你是不是受伤了呢？
      - be acquainted with 熟悉
      - be engaged 从事/订婚
         - My father is engaged in business. 我爸爸从事生意
         - Jonn is engaged to Mary. Jonn 和 Mary 订婚了
      - be used/accustomed to 习惯

         - I am used to staying up late at night. 我习惯在晚上熬夜（注意这里的 to 是介词）
         - used to v. 过去习惯于
         - be used to Ving. 现在习惯于
         - be used to v. 被用于...
      - be seated 坐下
         - Please be seated => Please seat yourself. 请坐下



   21. 助动词
      - 助动词不可连用
         - You will can swim soon. (x) 你很快能够游泳
         - You can be able to swim soon. (o) 你很快能够游泳

      - can-could 表 “能力，许可，请求，推测的可能”
         1. 表“能力” You can finish it. 你能够完成它
         2. 表“许可” You can come in if you have a stricket. 如果你有通行证，你可以进入
         3. 表“请求” Can you do me a favor? 你能帮我个忙吗？
         4. 表推测的“可能” It cannot be true. 它不可能是真的

      - may-might 表 “许可，推测的可能，诉愿”
         1. 表“许可” You may not chew gum in class. 上课时你不可以嚼口香糖
         2. 表推测的“可能”
            1. You may be right but I am against your opinion. 你可能是对的，但是我反对你的意思
            2. You might not know that you are here. 她不可能知道你在这里
               - 这里的 might 不是 may 的过去式，而是因为 might 作“可能”时，可能性比 may 较低
         3. 表“祈愿”
            1. May I never see asight like that agin. 愿我不会再见到那样的影像了
            2. May you succeed. 祝你成功（这里的 succeed 是动词）
               - succeed (v.)
               - success (n.)
               - successful (adj.)

      - must 表“义务，命令，推测的可能”
         1. must 必须，must not 不可以
            1. You must return the book by next Tuesday. 下周二前你必须归还这本书
            2. You mustn't talk when you eat. 你不可以在嘴巴有东西的时候说话
            3. Must I really attend the meeting. 我真的要出席这个会议吗
         2. must = have to；过去式则用 had to；未来式则用 will have to；完成式则用 have had to
            1. I must go to see the doctor because I caught a cold. 我必须去看医生，因为我得了感冒
               造价于：I have to go to see the doctor because I caught a cold.
         3. 注意口语中的 have got to（这里的 have 有助动词作用） 代替 have to（这里的 have 是一般动词）
            1. You've got to be more patient,Mary 你必须有更多耐心，玛莉
         4. 表“推测”
            1. She's very young.She cannot be over twenty. 她非常年轻，她可能不会超过二十岁
         5. 表“必然”All men must die. 所有的人一定会死

      - will 未来式
         1. 表现在的推测 
            1. Mom will be downstairs now. 妈妈可能在楼下
            2. That will be his house. 那可能是他的房子吧
         2. 表主词强烈的意志；固执（讲这句话的人很坚定）
            1. The window will not open. 窗户一定没开
         3. 表习惯；倾向
            1. Accidents will happen. 事故会发生
         4. 表请求；劝诱
            1. Will you bring me a ladder? 你拿个样子给我好吗
            2. Will you have one more coffee? 你要再来一杯咖啡吗

      - would
         1. 表“客气的请求”
            - mind + Ving / mind if + S + V
            - Would you mind if I sit next to you? 你介意我坐到在你旁边吗
               - Would you mind me sitting next to you?
               - Would you mind my sitting next to you?
         2. 表 过去的习惯 
            - Before they had television,people would listen to the radio. 在电视之前，众习惯听收音机
               - 加了 would 才明白有电视之前，众常做的是什么
            - Susan would often chat with us at this coffee shop. 苏姗经常在咖啡厅和我们聊天
         3. 表 过去的意志；固执
            - You wouldn't eat carrots when you were a boy. 当你是个孩子时，你怎么都不吃胡萝卜的
            - The rusty screw wouldn't come loose. 生锈的镙丝怎样都拧不动

      - used to
         1. 注意 would 和 used to 都表示过去习惯时
            - used to 表示的是过去相当长时间的习惯
            - would 表示过去动作的重复，习惯的意味比较淡
         2. 不同形式表达
            - 否定：used not to / didn't use to
            - 疑问：used + S + to / Did + S + use to
            - 举例
               - He used not to drink = He didn't use to drink. 他以前不习惯喝酒
               - Used he to drink? = Did he use to drink? 他以前喝酒吗

      - should
         - 表“义务”
            - You should study hard. 你应该努力学习
         - 表“推测”
            - He should be here within an hour. 他应该在一个小时内会到吧
         - 表强烈的惊奇 -- “怎么会”
            - Who should make such a foolish mistake. 谁会犯如此愚蠢的错误呢（意思即没有人会犯那样愚蠢的错误）

      - ought to
         - 较 should 较强，在口语中常用
         - My bicycle ought to be here.because I left it just beside the telegraph pole. 我脚踏车应该在这里，因为我就把它停在电线杆旁
         - You oughtn't to do so. 你不应该做那样的事情

      - need
         - need(v.) + 人 + need + to + v.
         - need(v.) + 物 + need(s) + Ving / to be p.p.
         - 你不需要说这么大声，我听得很清楚
            - You don't need to speak so loud,I hear you very well. （这里的 need 作一般动词）
            - You needn't speak so loud,I hear you very well. （这里的 need 作助动词）
         - 我需要把节目录下来吗？不，你不需要
            - Do I need to record the program on the tape? -- No,you don't need to.
            - Need I record the program? -- No,you needn't

      - dare
         - 过去式 dared，
         - dare 有一般动词，助动词 的用法（作助动词时一般是用于 否定 和 疑问句中）
         - dare 作动词时用法形成 dare (to) + 原型动词
         - 她敢说实话
            - She dare not tell the truth. 她不敢说实话（这里的 dare 是助动词）
            - She dares (to) tell the truth. 她敢说实话（这里的 dare 是一般动词）

      - 助动词的惯用表现
         1. can 的惯用表现
            - cannot help but v. / cannot help + Ving 禁不住；忍不住
            - cannot...too... 无论再...也不为过
               - You cannot be too careful when you choose a husband. 当你在选丈夫的时候，你无论再小心也不为过
         2. may(might) 的惯用表现
            1. may well + v. 大可，恐怕是
               - You may well say so. 她大可以这么说
            2. may(might) as well + v. 不妨
               - You'll never solve that problem.You might as well give up. 你绝不会解决这个问题，不妨放弃吧
            3. may(might) as well v. as v. 与其...不如...（这两件事情都是不好的事情，只不过一个稍好一点）
               - You might as well throw your money into the sea as lend it to him. 你与其把钱借给他不如把钱丢到海里

         3. should 用于 that 子句中时
            1. 子句表“感情因素”
               1. 常用的形容词有：surprising,strange,lucky,sad 等
               2. 举例
                  1. It is natwal that she should want to have children. 她想要小孩是自然的
                  2. It is a pity that he should be sick in bed. 他躺在床上是很遗憾的
                     - 也可以说成 It's a pity that he <should> is sick in bed.
                     - 此处没有加 should，但是加了 should 的感情因素更强烈些
                     - 此处如果加了 should，则 is 改为 be
            2. 子句表“当然，必要”时，
               1. should 通常省略，从句中不管主词是否是第三人称，动词都应该用原型（因为是大家都清楚的情况，只是省略了 should，但并不表示它不存在）
               2. 常用形容词的有：important,necessary,essential,right 等
               3. 举例
                  1. It is necessary that you (should) take this medicine right away. 你必须吃药是有必要的
                  2. It is important that you (should) remember this formula.
            3. 子句表“提案，要求”时
               1. should 通常可以省略，从句中不管主词是否是第三人称，动词都应该用原型（因为是大家都清楚的情况，所以省略）
               2. 常用形容词的有：advise,decide,demand,insist,order 等
               3. 举例
                  1. I suggest that the meeting (should) be postponce.
                  2. Mother insist that my brother (should) get up at five every morning.

         4. would 的惯用表现
            - would rather v. than ... 宁愿...不愿...
               - I would rather go today than yesterday. 我宁愿今天走也不愿明天走
            - would like to v. 想要
               - would like to Ving.
               - would like n.
               - I would like to have a date with her. 我想和她来次约会
                  - 等价：I feel like having a date with her.
                  - 等价：I want to have a date with her.

   22. 特殊构句
      1. 倒装句
         1. 倒装句的三种形式
            1. v. + S
            2. 助动词 + S + v.
            3. 助动词 + v. + S

         2. 否定定放句首
            1. 格式
               1. 否定字 + beV + S
               2. 否定字 + 助动词 + S + v.
            2. 否定字放句首
               1. He is never late for school. => Never is he late for school.
               2. He rarely tells a joke. => Rarely does he tell a joke.
               3. He had no sooner reached his house than it began to rain. 他一回到家，然后就开始下雨了
                  - No sooner had he reached his house than it began to rain.
                  - no sooner...than... 一...就...
               4. I didn't know (that) he was homesick until I talked to him. 直到我根他阿尔聊过才知道他得了思乡病
                  - Not until I talked to him did I know (that) he was homesick.
               5. He not only draws illustrations but he (also) writes novels.（这句话后半句 he 可以省略，因为前面出现过，并且 but 可以直接根动词）
                  - Not only does he draw illustrations but he (also) writes novels.

            3. only 在意义上近乎否定定，故可放句首，其后接倒装句
               - 这里只是动词本身是否定字，不是倒装句
               - Only in case of emergency can we use this exit. 只有在紧急善下才能使用这个出口
               - Only when it rains do we feel cool. 只有下雨时才觉得凉爽一些

            4. only if 和 if only
               1. Only if + 副词子句，倒装句...（只要）
               2. If only + 假没法过去式/过去完成式（要是...就好了）
               3. Only if you pass the examination and become a college student,will I accept you as a friend.
               4. If only I had money,I could buy some new clothes. 要是我有钱，我就可以买新衣服了

         3. 程度副词放句首
            1. 格式
               1. 方向/场所副词 + 一般动词/be动词 + S
               2. 方向/场所副词 + S + 一般动词/be动词
            2. 举例
               1. The teacher comes here. = Here comes the teacher. = Here the teacher comes. 这个老师来这里了
               2. A shower of stones came over the wall. = Over the wall came A shower of stones. 像阵雨般的石头越过墙壁
            
         4. 程度副词
            1. 格式
               1. 程度副词 + beV + S
               2. 程度副词 + 助动词 + v.
            2. 举例
               1. I remember well the promise you made last year. 我清楚记得你去年许下的承诺
                  - Well do I remember the promise you made last year.
               2. so...that...,such...that.... 也可以改成倒装句
                  - Jane is so beautiful that I am head over heels in love with her. Jane如此美丽，以至于我彻底爱上她
                     - So beautiful is Jane that I am head over heels in love with her.
                  - It was such a boring lecture that half the students fell asleep. 一堂好无聊的课，以至于半数学生都想睡觉
                     - Such a boring lecture was it that half the students fell asleep.

         5. 补语放在句首
            - 补语 + beV + S（这里的 beV 也可以是连辍动词）
               - The country that has no war is happy. = Happy is the country that has no war.
               - The sound of the bell grew faint. = Faint grew the sound of the bell. 钟声渐隐
            - 若主词是代名词则不能用倒装
               - He is happy. = Happy he is.（因为这里的 he 是代名词，所以没有倒装）

         6. 句中特别部分的强调
            - 概念
               - It beV 强调部分 that 剩余部分
               - 强调部分不能是：动词，形容词
               - 这里的 that 不能省略
            - 举例
               - My father caught a carp in this river last week. 我的爸爸上周在河里抓到一条鲤鱼
                  - 强调“my father”：It was my father that [who] caught a carp in this river last week.
                  - 强调“a carp”：It was a carp that [which] my father caught in this river last week.

         7. 动词，形容词，副词的强调
            1. 动词的强调 => do,does,did
               - Do feel free to call me at any time. 在任何时候打电话给我都可以（注意这里的 do 是强调 feel 的）
               - Shre rarely speaks at our meeting,but when she does speak,she is always to the point. 她很少在会议上说话，当她说话时，她往往讲到重点（注意这里的 does 是强调 speak 的）
            2. 强调名词 => 反身代名词或 very 修饰
               - This is the very spot where I found the purse. 这就是我找到这个钱包的地点（这里的 very作强调）
               - He is happiness itself. 他非常幸福（这里的 itself 强调 He 本身）
                  - He is very happy.
                  - He is all happiness.
            3. 强调形容词或副词
               - 词型
                  - 原型：very,so,awfully,hightly,terribly,dreadfully
                  - 比较级：much,even,a lot,far,still
                  - 最高级：much,far and away,by far,possible
               - 举例
                  - The dog is terribly smart. 这只狗非常聪明
                  - The question was much easier than I had thought. 这个问题比我想的容易多了
            4. 否定句的强调
               - 否定词提到句前，并作倒装
                  - 倒装为：beV/助动词 + S
               - 举例
                  - We will never make such a mistake again. 我将不会再犯这样的错误
                     - Never will we make such a mistake again.
                  - The dress does not suit her. 这件洋装不适合她
                     - Not does the dress suit her.


   23. 省略
      1. 词性的省略（一般用于大家都清楚 或 有重复的情况）
         1. I like her better than (I like) him. 我喜欢这个男生胜过喜欢这个女生
         2. I like her better than he. 我比他更喜欢这个女生
         3. (It) sounds great. 它听起来不错
      2. 子句与主句中有重复时，则省略（省略要考虑对应的动词是否需要省略）
         1. When (they were) in school,they were good friends.
         2. Though (he is) poor,he is an honest man.
         3. 注意 if 之后，所省略的主词及动词，可能和主要子句不同
            1. Please point out the mistakes if (there are) any (mistakes).
            2. If (meat) put in the refrigerator,meat will keep for a week.
      3. 子句与主句中有重复时，则省略（省略要考虑对应的动词是否需要省略）
         1. How careless (it is) of you to lose your wallet. 搞丢你的钱包多么粗心啊
         2. 感叹句中的 形容词 和副词 有时也可以省略
            - How (hard) it rains! 雨下得好大啊！
               - 雨下得怎么样，在不明确的情况下是不可以省略的，比如只是在书面表达，那么就不清楚省略的是什么意思了
            - What a (wounderful/terrible) day!（同理）


   24. 代名词
      - at all
         - 全然 => 用于否定句
         - 究竟 => 用于疑问句
         - 既然 => 用于条件句（if 子句）
      - 疑问
         - I wasn't surprised at all. 我不全然讶异
         - Do you know her at all? 你究竟认识她吗
         - If you learn English at all,learn it well. 既然你要学英语，学好它
         - For all her efforts,she failed. 尽管她很努力，但还是失败了

    25. 注意
       - 未列举完
          - some...,and some...（可数，不可数）
          - some...,and others...（可数）
       - 已列举完
          - some...,and the rest...（可数，不可数）
          - some...,and the others...（可数）

    26. 注意
       - had nothing to do with 与...有关
       - had much to do with 与...很有关
       - had little to do with 与...有一点关
